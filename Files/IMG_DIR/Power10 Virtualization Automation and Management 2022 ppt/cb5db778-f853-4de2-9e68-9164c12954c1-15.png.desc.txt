### Title:
- **IBM Cloud Management Console (CMC)**

### Description:
- The IBM Cloud Management Console (CMC) is described as a SaaS-based manager that provides a consolidated view of Power-based deployments across multiple regions and datacenters.
- It offers a comprehensive inventory of systems and virtualized resources, consolidated performance data to optimize utilization and performance across multiple Power-based data centers, and aggregated logging information for additional insights.

### Key Features:
1. **Patch Planning:**
   - Consolidated, easy-to-navigate views of code levels across your Power environment and available updates.
   - Create a patch plan with all stakeholders.

2. **Performance:**
   - Monitor the performance of your Power environment.
   - For systems, partitions, and shared storage pools, view real-time metrics and monitor data center efficiency with energy metrics.

3. **Inventory:**
   - Monitor the health status of all the resources in your Power environment.
   - Refresh and update your resource inventory to find what you need.
   - Select any resource to filter the status information for that resource.

4. **Logging:**
   - Use the Logging application to get insights into your data center.
   - View PowerVM Virtualization actions such as Live Migration, Snapshot, Remote Restart (RPR), and Partition Lifecycle.

### Additional Information:
- CMC is delivered as a SaaS offering from the IBM Cloud.
- It provides convenient pay-as-you-go multi-cloud management.
- A one-year CMC entitlement is included with Power E1080 servers.
